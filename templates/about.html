About This Archive

This archive contains documents related to government records that have been OCR'd (Optical Character Recognition) to make their contents searchable.

About the Collection

This collection includes documents from government archives that have been processed to extract their text content. This makes it possible to search through thousands of pages of documents quickly and efficiently.

How to Search

Simply enter your search terms in the search box on the home page. You can search for:

  • Single words (e.g., "Kennedy")
  • Multiple words (e.g., "Robert Kennedy")
  • Phrases in quotes (e.g., "assassination investigation")

Technical Information

This search interface is powered by GovDocHarvester, an open-source tool for downloading, OCR processing, and searching government document collections. The documents have been processed with Tesseract OCR, and the search functionality uses the Whoosh search engine.

Limitations

Please note that OCR technology is not perfect, especially when dealing with scanned documents of varying quality. There may be errors in the extracted text, so if you don't find what you're looking for, try alternative search terms or spellings.
