Date 6/17/68

ALOYSIUS H, ZUGGER, Vice-President and General
Manager of the Ambassador Hotel, advised as follows:

He was present in the Ambassador Hotel the whole
day of June 4, 1968. On the evening of June 4, 1968, he
was in all of the banquet rooms. He was in the Embassy
Room during Senator ROBERT KENNEDY's victory speech and he
left just before the speech ended and went home. He arrived
at his home, which is on the grounds, at about 12:15 AM
on June 5, 1968. After he had been home several minutes,
he heard a siren near the hotel and he went back to the
hotel. He went into the kitchen behind the Embassy Room
at about 12:30 AM, and he saw neither SIRHAN SIRHAN nor
Senator KENNEDY. ZUGGER was shown a photograph of SIRHAN
STRHAN and he advised that he has never seen the man.
